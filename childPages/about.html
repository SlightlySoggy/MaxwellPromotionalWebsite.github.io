About Maxwell

Background

Introduction

My name is Maxwell Li. I am a highschool student of Lillian Osborne who has close ties to the community. I find joy in learning and volunteering. However on days when I can't find the motivation to work, I entertain myself with a huge collection of videogames, anime, books, and television shows.

School

You may know me from the roles that I have in the community and in my school. I currently serve as both the Grade 11 Representative and Student Senate of Lillian Osborne Highschool.

Community

I also have a large range of volunteer experience. I enjoy helping the community, the ways I demonstrate this include over 300 hours of volunteer work at the Edmonton Food Bank. I also enjoy taking part in local politics and providing opportunities, for that reason I created a student based volunteer organisasion that currently has 50 members. We helped Edmonton Public School Board Trustee Nathan Ip canvass during his, now victorious, nomination contest.
