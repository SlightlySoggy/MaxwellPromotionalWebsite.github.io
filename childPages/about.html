A Promotional Website For Maxwell Li

About Maxwell

Description

This my submission for the promotional website project. This website contains a navigation bar and four pages.

About

My name is Maxwell Li. I currently reside in Edmonton and go to Lillian Osborne Highschool. I'm known for my role as Lillian Osborne student senate member and my role as Grade 11 Student Representative. I have already served as student representative for the 2021 - 2022 school year and was re-elected as student representative. Hear more about me and my interests in the About Page.

Future

This website, as you can tell, is far from being finished. Future updates and work will be done on this website. Here you will be able to see posts about my future projects and progress through my life and my current roles. Some things that I currently have planned include an interactive comment board and my posts page.
